How to Play

Ladigue Island Quest is a short interactive story. You read each scene and choose what to do next. Each choice takes you to a new part of the story, and different decisions can lead to different endings.

  1. Start: Go to the Play page and begin the story.
  2. Choose: Click one of the choice buttons to decide what happens next.
  3. Explore: Replay to find new routes and unlock more endings.
  4. Restart: Use the Restart button to begin again anytime.
  5. Sound: Use the Sound toggle to enable/disable click sound effects.

Accessibility & Tips

  • All buttons and links can be used with a keyboard (Tab / Enter).
  • Text is designed to be readable with clear contrast and spacing.
  • If something looks stuck, click Restart or return to Home.
